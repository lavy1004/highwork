Problems for General Backers

  • Absence of discrimination or
    verification means

    Investors generally find it difficult to identify
    which startup or product to sponsor,
    as it is very difficult for them to
    collect information by themselves.
    These circumstances make it is difficult
    for the general investor to make informed
    investment decisions.

  • Absence of communication or
    feedback environment

    It is difficult to communicate with the
    project and there are no good feedback
    environment.

  • Non-responsibility structure of
    Crowdfunding Platform

    For general investors, even if the investment
    loss is caused by lack of verification information
    and communication environment,
    the responsibility structure of the intermediary
    is very poor.

Problems for Startups and Projects

  • Difficulties in
    marketing preparation

    Many startups or projects have no experience
    with crowdfunding. They also have difficult
    time with self-diagnosis of whether the
    projects or products are good model for
    crowdfunding.

  • Difficulties in
    financing

    Even with the groundbreaking items and
    promising team members, it's hard to fund
    the project. Despite the completion of
    development in the technical aspect,
    marketing or branding is difficult, so the
    business cannot be greatly expanded.

  • Difficulty in accessibility
    after financing stage

    Even if the funding is successful,
    it is difficult to find out appropriate
    potential partners for the project to
    grow and develop in specific market area.

KEY FEATURES of HIGHWORK ECOSYSTEM

  • 01. Application step

    Anyone can apply to the HighWork Crowdfunding Platform
    by filling in the application form.

  • 02. Excavation & Pre-Validation Step

    Initially filter out malicious participants or projects or products that
    do not reach basic requirements. In a centralized manner, filtering is
    provided to provide an efficient and reasonable voting environmen
    for HighWork crowdfunding platform participants.

    In this case, machine learning technology can be introduced to
    prepare tools for selecting projects or products that do not meet the
    basic requirements.

  • 03. Listing Step

    If the project passes first pre-validation, HighWork Crowdfunding
    Platform Service will provide general investors with a concise and
    clear view of information of various projects.

  • 04. Crowdfunding

    This is the stage where decentralization takes place. In the past,
    if a project or product was funded in a centralized way, the HighWor
    Crowdfunding Platform is a collective intelligence force that allows
    ordinary investors to voting on projects or products they want to
    fund through HighWork Token.

Overview of HIGHWORK Token Economy

Highwork token (HWT) is a utility token of the HighWork blockchain platform. HWT is an ERC-20 compatible token with 18 decimal places. HWT will be used as a credit for investing projects listed within the HighWork Platform, and this ecosystem will continue to increase the value of the HighWork Token.

Key Reward Types of HWT Economy

Key rewards for investors on the High Work Platform include:
1) project funding rewards, 2) community activity rewards, and
3) bounty program rewards.

Highwork Funding Process

Investors can invest in projects or products listed on the HIGHWORK platform via cash or cryptocurrency. At this time, various investment benefits exist when investing through HWT. In other words, in the case of blockchain projects, investors can buy HWT through the market and then invest in issuance tokens of funding pro ects, which are pegged with HWT at a certain rate. The investment through HWT is securely deposited through a study wallet or other financial institution linked to the HIGHWORK platform.

Business Model

Funding fees are HighWork's largest business model. A certain percentage of project funding costs can be earned as a platform usage fee to achieve a certain level of operating profit. In addition, it is possible to take the initiative in the flow of tokens by securing interest income and reserves arising from differences in time of payment.
